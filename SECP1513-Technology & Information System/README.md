### What This Course Covers

Technology & Information Systems provides a comprehensive exploration of how IT infrastructure supports modern organizations. From understanding physical hardware components to designing complex databases and secure networks, this course bridges theoretical knowledge with practical business applications. Students examine how information systems drive organizational efficiency and strategic decision-making.
