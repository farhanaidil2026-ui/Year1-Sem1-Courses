### What This Course Covers

Technology & Information Systems gives a comprehensive look at how IT infrastructure supports modern organizations. We covered everything from physical hardware components to database design and network security. The course does a great job connecting theoretical concepts with practical business applications, showing us how information systems actually drive organizational efficiency and help with strategic decision-making.
